Greetings! My name is Deacon Carreon, and I am a Physics & Astrophysics double major at UC Berkeley. I am currently doing research at the Space Sciences Laboratory that analyzes data with the MAVEN Solar Energetic Particle (SEP) instruments on an extrasolar x-ray source. I have also worked as a research & blogs writer and intreviewer for the Berkeley Scientific Journal I am also an MPS mentor, working to pass down the knowledge that I have acquired as an undergrad to others so they can succeed at what they do. My current goal after undergrad is to enter an Astrophysics Ph.D. program, and possibly join NASA or academia after that. In my free time, I like to read sci-fi and science books, play the piano, watch movies, and travel through cities and nature alike.
